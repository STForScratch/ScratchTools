ScratchTools Support

This is the ScratchTools Support Center. You can read any important documentation or directly contact the support team.

Terms & Conditions

The ScratchTools support center is meant to help users who need help using ScratchTools or getting started. The developer team tries its best to make the settings page and features intuitive, but it can still be difficult for some users to get used to at first.

When using support chats, please make sure to be respectful. Don't spam, and only open chats if you are unable to find help/information on a ScratchTools-related issue. Please make sure to be patient with support members on the other end.

Please be aware that support members have access to your Scratch username, enabled features/settings, ScratchTools version, and also have the ability to update settings for you.
